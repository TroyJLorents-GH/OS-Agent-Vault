# AI-powered study tools

AI-powered study tools leverage artificial intelligence to assist students in creating, organizing, and enhancing study materials. These tools often incorporate features such as automated content generation, interactive quizzes, flashcards, and chat-based assistance to streamline the learning process.

Adobe recently launched **Acrobat Spaces**, a free AI-powered study tool designed to help students create presentations, flashcards, quizzes, and other study resources from various document types without requiring a login. Developed with input from students at universities like Harvard, Berkeley, and Brown, Acrobat Spaces aims to be a comprehensive hub for study activities. It integrates AI features including chat assistance and AI-generated podcasts, positioning itself as a competitor to similar platforms such as Google's [[NotebookLM]] and [[Goodnotes]].

Acrobat Spaces is hosted on a separate URL to encourage adoption and ease of access. Adobe’s approach reflects a growing trend in educational technology to incorporate AI capabilities that enhance productivity and personalized learning experiences. This tool complements Adobe’s broader suite of creative and productivity applications, including [[Adobe Express]] and Turbo AI.
