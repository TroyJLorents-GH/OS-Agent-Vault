# Perplexity

AI-powered search engine that answers questions with inline citations. Crossed 1 billion monthly queries in Q1 2026. (Source: [[S — Best AI Tools 2026]])

## Key Features

- Combines real-time web search with AI synthesis to deliver sourced answers
- Citation model — every claim links back to its source, building trust in a way [[ChatGPT]] and [[Claude]] don't by default
- Carves a unique niche between traditional search engines and AI chatbots
- Free tier available / $20/mo Pro

## Why It Matters

Perplexity is essentially real-time [[Retrieval-Augmented Generation]] over the open web. Rather than relying solely on training data (with its staleness and hallucination risks), it retrieves live sources and synthesizes answers on the fly. The citation model makes it essential for research tasks where traceability matters.

The 1 billion monthly queries milestone signals that users are shifting real search volume away from Google toward AI-native search interfaces.

## Connections

- [[Retrieval-Augmented Generation]] — Perplexity is a product-scale implementation of RAG over the live web
- [[AI Grounding in Source Documents]] — the citation-first approach is a practical solution to grounding
- [[Claude]] — competes in the "answer my question" space, but Perplexity's web access and citations give it a research edge
- [[Google]] — the existential competitive threat; Perplexity is what Google search would be if rebuilt from scratch around LLMs
