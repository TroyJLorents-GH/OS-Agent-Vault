# ElevenLabs

Leading AI voice platform. Text-to-speech quality is essentially indistinguishable from human voice. (Source: [[S — Best AI Tools 2026]])

## Key Features

- Text-to-speech with near-human fidelity
- Voice cloning that is eerily accurate from short samples
- Supports dozens of languages
- Free tier available / $5–22/mo paid plans

## Why It Matters

ElevenLabs owns the voice AI market in 2026. If a workflow involves any form of audio content — [[Podcasts]], videos, audiobooks, voice interfaces, accessibility features — ElevenLabs is the default tool. The quality gap between ElevenLabs and competitors is wide enough that it functions as a near-monopoly in the space.

## Relevance to Troy's Projects

Worth considering for any product that needs voice UI or audio output. Potential applications include:

- Voice-narrated study materials or flashcard review (relevant to educational tools)
- Audio interfaces for agent-based products
- Content creation pipelines that output podcast or video narration
