# Adobe Express

Adobe Express is a free AI-powered study tool developed by Adobe, designed to help students create a variety of study materials such as presentations, flashcards, quizzes, and mind maps from multiple document types. It offers an integrated platform that combines document upload and processing with AI features, including chat assistance and AI-generated podcasts, to enhance the study experience.

The tool aims to serve as a comprehensive hub for students by streamlining the creation and organization of educational content without requiring users to log in. Adobe developed Adobe Express with input from students at leading universities to ensure a user-centered design tailored to academic needs. It is hosted on a separate URL to encourage adoption and ease of access.

Adobe Express competes with similar AI-powered study tools like [[NotebookLM]] by Google and [[Goodnotes]], positioning itself as a versatile solution in the educational technology space. Its AI grounding in source documents ensures that generated content remains accurate and relevant, supporting effective learning.
