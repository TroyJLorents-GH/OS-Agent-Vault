# Claude.ai

Claude.ai is an advanced conversational AI developed by Anthropic, designed to engage users through natural language interactions. It serves as a platform for gathering and analyzing qualitative insights on AI usage, enabling a deeper understanding of user experiences, aspirations, and concerns across diverse demographics.

Anthropic conducted the largest and most multilingual qualitative study on AI usage through Claude.ai, collecting data from over 80,000 users in 159 countries and 70 languages. This extensive research explored users' hopes, fears, and real-world interactions with AI, categorizing their primary desires into nine key themes. These themes include professional excellence, personal transformation, societal impact, creative expression, and more, reflecting a complex balance of optimism and apprehension about AI’s integration into daily life.

The study highlights the nuanced perspectives users hold toward AI, moving beyond theoretical debates to focus on concrete user experiences. Claude.ai’s role in this research underscores its capability as a conversational AI interviewer, facilitating open-ended interviews and enabling sophisticated AI classification and sentiment analysis.

Applications of Claude.ai extend to AI-assisted productivity, personal growth, entrepreneurship, societal challenges, learning, and creative assistance. By capturing diverse user inputs, Claude.ai contributes to the development of AI systems that better align with human values and needs.

Claude.ai exemplifies the intersection of AI technology and qualitative research, providing valuable insights that inform the ethical and practical advancement of AI-driven tools.
