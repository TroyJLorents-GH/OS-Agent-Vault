# Midjourney

Leading AI image generator known for unmatched artistic and aesthetic quality. (Source: [[S — Best AI Tools 2026]])

## Key Features

- **Version 8**: native 2K resolution images, 5x faster generation than v7
- Unmatched richness, depth, and artistic coherence across styles
- $10–60/mo depending on plan tier
- Discord-based interface (polarizing — power users love it, newcomers find it unintuitive)

## Strengths and Weaknesses

**Strengths:** Gold standard for visual quality. For anything artistic or creative, Midjourney is the starting point. The aesthetic coherence and "painterly" quality remain ahead of competitors.

**Weaknesses:** Garbles text rendered inside images. For any use case requiring legible text in generated images, [[Ideogram]] is the better choice.

## Connections

- [[Ideogram]] — complementary tool; use Ideogram when text rendering matters
- [[DALL-E]] — competitor from [[OpenAI]]; historically less artistic but improving
- The image generation space is maturing rapidly — Midjourney's lead is in taste and aesthetics, not raw capability
