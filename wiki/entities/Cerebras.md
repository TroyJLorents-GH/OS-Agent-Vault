# Cerebras

AI chipmaker known for **wafer-scale chips** — single silicon wafers used as one massive chip, reducing latency and simplifying parallelism for AI workloads. Pitched as alternative to [[entities/NVIDIA]] GPU stack.

## 2026 IPO (May 15)

- **IPO price:** $185
- **Open:** $350 (+89%)
- **Close:** $331.07 (+68%)
- **Raise:** ~$5.5B
- **Fully diluted valuation:** ~$106.75B at open
- **Close market cap:** ~$95B
- **Significance:** Largest US tech IPO since Uber (2019); first pure-play public bet on AI hardware alternatives to Nvidia

## Strategic Position

- **Wafer-scale pitch:** reduce latency, simplify AI workloads vs networked-GPU clusters
- **Bet:** chip scarcity + model scaling + sovereign AI strategies create demand for Nvidia alternatives
- **Risk:** Nvidia's CUDA ecosystem moat unchanged; software lock-in is the harder problem than silicon
- **Public-market readout:** investors pricing AI chip startups as strategic infrastructure, not software multiples
