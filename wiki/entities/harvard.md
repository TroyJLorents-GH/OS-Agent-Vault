# Harvard

Harvard University is a private Ivy League research university located in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Founded in 1636, it is the oldest institution of higher education in the United States. Harvard is renowned for its rigorous academic programs, distinguished faculty, and extensive library system. It offers undergraduate, graduate, and professional degrees across a wide range of disciplines.

Harvard's campus includes multiple schools such as Harvard College, Harvard Business School, Harvard Law School, and Harvard Medical School. The university is known for its significant contributions to research, innovation, and public service. It maintains a global reputation for academic excellence and competitive admissions.

Harvard's influence extends beyond academia through its alumni network, which includes numerous Nobel laureates, heads of state, and leaders in various fields. The university also fosters interdisciplinary collaboration and hosts numerous research centers and institutes.

As a leading institution, Harvard is often referenced in discussions about [[Ivy League]], [[higher education]], and [[research universities]] in the United States.
