# Ivan Mehta

Ivan Mehta is a product leader known for his work in the development of AI-powered educational tools. He played a key role in Adobe's launch of Acrobat Spaces, a free AI-driven study platform designed to help students create presentations, flashcards, quizzes, and other study materials from various document types without requiring login.

Under Mehta's guidance, Acrobat Spaces integrates advanced AI features such as chat assistance and AI-generated podcasts, positioning the product as a comprehensive hub for student learning. The tool competes with other AI study aids like [[NotebookLM]] by Google and [[Goodnotes]]. Mehta emphasized a user-centered design approach, incorporating feedback from students at top universities to tailor the platform to real academic needs.

The product is hosted on a separate URL to encourage adoption and ease of access, reflecting Mehta's focus on accessibility and seamless user experience. His work on Acrobat Spaces highlights the growing intersection of [[AI]], [[educational-technology]], and document processing in modern study tools.
