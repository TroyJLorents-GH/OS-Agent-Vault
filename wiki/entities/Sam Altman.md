# Sam Altman

CEO and co-founder of [[entities/OpenAI]]. Central figure in 2026 AI industry; named defendant in [[entities/Elon Musk]]'s lawsuit alleging OpenAI abandoned its nonprofit roots.

## Musk v. OpenAI Trial (May 2026)

- Musk seeks removal of Altman + Greg Brockman as part of $150B damages claim
- Musk's lawyer accused Altman of **lying based on witness testimony** during closing arguments
- OpenAI's defense: Musk knew about for-profit plans since 2018 and wanted control of Tesla or xAI; described Musk as suffering "selective amnesia"
- **Outcome (2026-05-18):** jury **dismissed all claims** (statute of limitations); Altman and Brockman **cleared, not liable**. Musk to appeal. Altman exits the trial with governance intact ahead of the rumored $1T IPO. ([NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/05/18/nx-s1-5822366/musk-altman-openai-jury-verdict-claims-dismissed))
