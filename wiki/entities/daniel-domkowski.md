# Daniel Domkowski

Author and thought leader on AI infrastructure for scientific research. Writes for the Red Hat blog and Medium on topics including [[HPC-cloud convergence]], [[fine-tuning]], and [[domain-specific AI]] deployment in research institutions.

## Key Positions

- Advocates for converged HPC + cloud-native platforms as the foundation for institutional AI
- Argues that fine-tuned [[small language models]] are "mission instruments" compared to frontier models as "reasoning engines"
- Emphasizes that the institutions that move fastest will train smaller, focused models rather than just adopting LLMs
