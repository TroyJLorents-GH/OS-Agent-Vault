# Apple

Platform owner — iOS, macOS, App Store, Siri. Controls user interface, distribution, OS layer for ~1.5B active devices.

## AI Positioning (2026)

- **Siri + ChatGPT (2024 onwards):** integrated [[entities/OpenAI]] ChatGPT into Siri. Apple owns the UX shell; OpenAI provides the model.
- **OpenAI friction:** as of May 2026, [[entities/OpenAI]] reportedly exploring legal options against Apple over how the partnership has unfolded. Core tension: who controls the user relationship.
- **EU Digital Markets Act position:** Apple **backed [[entities/google]]'s pushback** against EU proposals requiring Android to give third-party AI services parity access with Gemini. Apple has parallel exposure on iOS — same regulatory pressure applies to Siri.

## Strategic Stakes

Mobile OS is the next AI distribution layer. Whoever controls **default assistants, app permissions, on-device data, system-level access** shapes which AI services reach billions of users. See [[AI Regulation]].
