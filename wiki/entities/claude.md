# Claude

Conversational AI assistant developed by [[Anthropic]]. Central to the 81K study both as the product users were reflecting on and as the infrastructure that made the study possible (via Anthropic Interviewer and Claude-powered classifiers).

## How Users Experience Claude (from 81K Study)

81% of users reported that AI had already taken a step toward their stated vision. The dominant use patterns:

- **Productivity tool** (32%) — technical acceleration, automating routine tasks
- **Cognitive partner** (17%) — brainstorming, thinking through problems, creative collaboration
- **Learning tool** (10%) — patient tutoring, adaptive explanations, skill development
- **Accessibility technology** (9%) — enabling non-developers to ship apps, disability support
- **Research synthesizer** (7%) — literature review, navigating complex information
- **Emotional companion** (6%) — judgment-free support, grief processing, mental health bridging

## Notable User Experiences

- A mute user built a text-to-speech bot for real-time communication
- Ukrainian programmer learned C# and SQL via Claude, securing an IT job and military deferment
- A butcher with 20+ years transitioned to tech entrepreneurship
- Multiple medical diagnoses found after years of misdiagnosis
- Soldiers in war zones using Claude as emotional anchor during shelling

## Known Issues (from Users)

- **[[Sycophancy]]** (10.8%) — too agreeable, reinforcing user delusions instead of pushing back
- **Unreliability** (26.7%) — hallucinations, fake citations, verification burden
- **Overrestriction** (11.7%) — excessive safety measures blocking legitimate use
- **Emotional dependency** — the most entangled tension in [[light and shade of AI]]

## Connections

- Built by [[Anthropic]] with explicit alignment focus
- User concerns map directly to alignment research priorities: [[sycophancy]], overrestriction, autonomy
- Functions as [[AI as equalizer]] — disproportionately empowering in developing countries and for people with disabilities
- Risk of [[cognitive atrophy]] in institutional (but not volitional) learning contexts
- Core component of [[Coding Agent Architecture]] via Claude Code (see [[S — Components of A Coding Agent]])
