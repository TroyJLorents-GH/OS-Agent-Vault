# Claude

Claude is an AI system developed by Anthropic, designed to engage users through conversational interactions. It was central to the largest and most multilingual qualitative study on AI usage, which gathered insights from over 80,000 users across 159 countries and 70 languages. The study aimed to understand users’ hopes, fears, and experiences with AI, providing a comprehensive view of how AI is perceived globally.

The research categorized primary user desires for AI into nine main themes, including professional excellence, personal transformation, societal impact, and creative expression. This classification highlights the complex coexistence of optimism and concern regarding AI’s role in everyday life. Claude, as a conversational AI interviewer, facilitated open-ended interviews that enabled nuanced collection of real-world user perspectives, moving beyond abstract debates to concrete experiences.

Anthropic’s study utilized methods such as [[qualitative research]], [[sentiment analysis]], and AI classification to analyze the data. Claude’s role extended to supporting AI-assisted productivity, personal growth, entrepreneurship, societal challenges, learning, and creative assistance. The insights derived emphasize the multifaceted aspirations and anxieties people hold about AI technologies, reflecting a diverse and global user base.
