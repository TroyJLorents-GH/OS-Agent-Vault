# Runway

Leading AI video generation tool and the professional's choice for AI-assisted video production. (Source: [[S — Best AI Tools 2026]])

## Key Features

- **Gen-4.5**: best-in-class temporal consistency, motion control, and narrative continuity across scenes
- Full post-production toolset beyond just generation
- $12–76/mo depending on plan tier
- [[OpenAI]] shut down Sora; Runway inherited the quality crown

## Competitive Landscape

| Tool | Strengths | Trade-offs |
|------|-----------|------------|
| **Runway Gen-4.5** | Best overall quality, production toolset | Premium pricing |
| **Kling 2.0** | ~40% of the cost, ~80% of the quality | Less polish |
| **Google Veo 3.1** | Best physics simulation | Narrower toolset |
| **Pika 2.0** | Fastest generation | Lower fidelity |

Runway is closest to a real production tool in the AI video space. The combination of generation quality and post-production features makes it the default for professional workflows.

## Connections

- [[OpenAI]] — Sora's shutdown left a vacuum that Runway filled
- [[Google]] — Veo 3.1 competes on physics accuracy but not overall production readiness
