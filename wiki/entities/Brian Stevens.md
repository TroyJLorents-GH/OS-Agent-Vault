# Brian Stevens

Author at [[Red Hat AI|Red Hat]] Blog. Wrote about the strategic necessity of a standard [[AI Operating System]] for enterprise AI deployment, drawing a direct analogy to how Linux standardized server infrastructure.

Stevens advocates for the Linux-like "coopetition" model for AI infrastructure — competitors collaborating on a shared open-source AI OS layer while competing commercially on top. His article frames [[vLLM]] as the "kernel" of AI inference and Kubernetes as the control plane, with llm-d providing distributed inference at scale.

**Key publication:** [Developing a standard AI OS: Unlocking production-grade AI at enterprise scale](https://www.redhat.com/en/blog/developing-standard-ai-os) (2025-08-14) — see [[S — Developing a Standard AI OS]].
