# Goodnotes

Goodnotes is a digital note-taking application designed primarily for students and professionals to create, organize, and annotate handwritten and typed notes. It supports various document formats, allowing users to import PDFs, images, and other files to enhance their note-taking experience. Goodnotes is widely used on tablets and touchscreen devices, leveraging stylus input for natural handwriting and drawing.

The app offers features such as searchable handwriting, customizable templates, and seamless synchronization across devices. It enables users to create structured notebooks, flashcards, and study materials, facilitating efficient learning and information management. Goodnotes competes with other AI-powered and digital study tools like Adobe Acrobat Spaces and Google’s NotebookLM by focusing on intuitive note organization and annotation capabilities rather than integrated AI assistance.

Goodnotes emphasizes user-friendly design and flexibility, catering to diverse workflows in education and professional settings. Its popularity stems from combining traditional handwriting benefits with digital convenience, making it a preferred choice for users seeking a versatile digital notebook.

[[Adobe Acrobat Spaces]] and [[Google NotebookLM]] represent emerging AI-enhanced alternatives that integrate features such as AI-generated flashcards, quizzes, and chat assistants, expanding the scope of digital study tools beyond note-taking.
