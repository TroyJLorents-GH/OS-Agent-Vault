# NVIDIA

NVIDIA is the essential infrastructure company for AI — its GPUs (H100, Blackwell, RTX) power most AI training and inference globally. Partner in virtually every AI product covered in the wiki, from frontier labs to startups to robotics platforms.

## Key 2026 Initiatives

- **Isaac GR00T open models** — humanoid robots understanding natural language
- **Alpamayo physical AI platform** — helping BMW, Ford on autonomous driving
- **Jetson Thor** — real-time sensor processing for humanoid robotics
- **Cosmos, GR00T open models** — physical AI foundation models
- **Isaac Lab-Arena** — standardized physical AI evaluation environment
- **NVIDIA GTC 2026:** dominated by enterprise agentic deployments — **NeMoCLAW** and **OpenCLAW** frameworks drew the largest attendance

## Partnerships

- **Red Hat** — NVIDIA is a core partner in the standard [[AI Operating System]] alongside Google, IBM, AMD, Intel, and Hugging Face (Source: [[S — Developing a Standard AI OS]])
- **Mistral** — Blackwell chip optimization for Ministral 3 throughput
- **BMW, Ford** — autonomous driving via Alpamayo
- **Consumer GPU stack** — optimized inference for Gemma 4 on RTX hardware, enabling local agentic AI on consumer-grade GPUs

## Strategic Position

### Training Monopoly Under Pressure

The **Huawei/GLM-5 training demonstration** (see [[S — Open-Source AI Landscape April 2026]]) is the **first credible challenge to NVIDIA's training monopoly** — Chinese labs can now train frontier models with zero NVIDIA dependency. This is the first structural crack in the training-side moat.

### Inference Moat Still Strong

NVIDIA's moat in **inference** (CUDA ecosystem, optimized kernels, vLLM integration, ecosystem lock-in) remains robust. See [[Inference Economics]] — as the industry pivots to inference as the primary spend, NVIDIA's serving-side advantages matter more.

### Pivot to Physical AI

NVIDIA is pivoting hard toward **physical AI and robotics** as [[Inference Economics]] pressures pure-LLM GPU margins. Isaac GR00T, Alpamayo, Jetson Thor, Cosmos, and Isaac Lab-Arena are all bets that **[[Physical AI]] becomes the next trillion-dollar compute market** — and that NVIDIA can dominate it the way it dominated LLM training.
