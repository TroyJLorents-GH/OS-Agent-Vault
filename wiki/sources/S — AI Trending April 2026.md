## Core Thesis

AI investment and adoption have reached escape velocity in early 2026. The industry is shifting from chatbots to [[Agentic AI]] — autonomous systems that execute complex tasks — while multimodal capabilities, healthcare AI, and financial AI are all scaling rapidly. The numbers are staggering: $242 billion in VC funding in Q1 2026 alone.

## Key Claims & Findings

### AI Investment Explosion

- **$242 billion** in venture capital invested in AI in Q1 2026 — representing **80% of all global venture funding**
- This is up from $59.6B in the same period in 2025 — roughly a **4x increase** year-over-year
- Global AI market valued at **$390.91B** in 2025, projected to reach **$539.45B** in 2026
- [[OpenAI]] raised **$120B+** by March 2026, valued at **$852B**, with 900 million weekly ChatGPT users
- [[Anthropic]] reached a **$380B valuation** after a $30B raise

### Agentic AI — The Dominant Trend

- [[Agentic AI]] is the biggest shift: AI systems that perform complex tasks autonomously, not just answer questions
- **Prediction:** the first **$1M business fully run by AI agents** will emerge by end of 2026
- Key product launches:
  - **Microsoft Copilot Cowork** — multi-model collaboration for enterprise workflows
  - **[[Anthropic]] Conway** — always-on agent for continuous task execution
  - **Salesforce Slackbot upgrade** — agentic capabilities in workplace messaging
- Links to [[Coding Agent Architecture]] and the broader [[AI Operating System]] vision

### Multimodal AI Advances

- **Google Gemini** — text + image generation capabilities expanding
- **xAI Grok Imagine** — generates 6-second video from text prompts
- Multimodal is becoming table stakes, not a differentiator

### Healthcare AI

- AI healthcare market projected to exceed **$45B+ by 2026**
- **Noah Labs Vox** — detects heart failure from a **5-second voice recording**
- **Epic** and **Oracle** rolling out AI-powered clinical documentation
- Healthcare represents one of the highest-ROI verticals for AI deployment

### Finance & Enterprise AI

- **Gartner** predicts **40% of business software** will include autonomous AI by end of 2026
- Generative AI could add **$200–340B to global banking profits**
- **Ford Pro AI** analyzes **1 billion data points daily** for fleet optimization

### April 2026 Specific Moves

- Google released **Gemma 4** (open model family)
- OpenAI confirmed $852B valuation milestone
- Microsoft upgraded Copilot for multi-model collaboration
- **EU AI Act** phased enforcement began in 2026
