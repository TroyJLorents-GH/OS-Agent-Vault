## 🔍 What is Agent OS? (Short Summary)

**Agent OS** is a foundational software platform — analogous to a traditional computer operating system — built specifically to **host, run, coordinate, and govern autonomous AI agents**. Rather than being a single product, "Agent OS" refers to a growing category of infrastructure. Notable implementations include:

- **AIOS** (open-source, from arXiv/AGI Research) — An LLM-based OS kernel for scheduling and managing AI agent processes
- **PwC's Agent OS** — An enterprise-grade platform for deploying and governing AI agents at scale
- **Rivet's agentOS** — A complete platform for running and operating AI agents
- **agentsos.sh / agentos.sh** — Platforms for building autonomous AI agents with adaptive intelligence
