**Key Question:**  
What is ambient computing?

**Findings:**  
Ambient computing refers to a technology paradigm where computing is integrated seamlessly into the environment, allowing devices and systems to interact with users in a natural, unobtrusive manner. It enables continuous, context-aware interaction through interconnected devices that respond intelligently to user needs without explicit commands.
