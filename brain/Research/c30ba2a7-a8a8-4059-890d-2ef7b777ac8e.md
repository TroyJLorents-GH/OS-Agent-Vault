**Definition of Ambient Computing:**  
Ambient computing refers to technology that seamlessly integrates into the environment, enabling devices and systems to interact with users naturally and contextually without explicit input. It leverages sensors, AI, and connectivity to create an intuitive, always-on computing experience embedded in everyday surroundings.
