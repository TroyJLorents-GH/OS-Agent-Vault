# Ivan Mehta

## Description

Adobe has launched Acrobat Spaces, a free AI-powered study tool designed for students to create presentations, flashcards, quizzes, and other study materials from various document types without requiring login. The tool aims to serve as a one-stop hub for students by integrating AI features such as chat assistance and AI-generated podcasts, competing with similar products like Google’s NotebookLM and Goodnotes. Adobe developed Acrobat Spaces with input from students at top universities and is hosting it on a separate URL to encourage adoption.
