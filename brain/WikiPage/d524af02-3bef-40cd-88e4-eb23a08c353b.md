# open-ended interviews

## Description

Anthropic conducted the largest and most multilingual qualitative study on AI usage, gathering insights from over 80,000 users across 159 countries and 70 languages about their hopes, fears, and experiences with AI. The study categorized users' primary desires for AI into nine main themes, ranging from professional excellence and personal transformation to societal impact and creative expression, highlighting a complex coexistence of optimism and concern about AI’s role in their lives. The research emphasizes the nuanced, real-world aspirations and anxieties people have about AI, moving beyond abstract debates to concrete user perspectives.
